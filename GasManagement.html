Gas Management

Energy Atlantica has over 50+ combined years of natural gas trading and scheduling experience in Canada an d the Northeast United States. We manage assets for large industrial and commercial clients, producers, power generators, and local distribution companies.

Our team of expert traders uses only the best tools available; a state-of-the-art trading floor, ICE and NGX, WSI Weather Forecasting, Platt’s, Reuters and Morningstar, Risk Management Policy and Systems.

Our Services include:

  • Economic dispatch
  • Daily gas trading and scheduling: late day, same day, and day ahead
  • Term trading and analysis
  • 24/7/365 coverage
  • Transportation optimization
  • Reporting and reconciliation

Case Study

The Client: A large industrial client that consumes between 3,500 and 30,000 MMBtu/day across 10+ facilities.

The Issue: The client had no visibility to the highly volatile and dynamic natural gas market, often missing opportunities to switch fuels when market conditions changed.

Services: For 14 years, we have provided economic dispatch for these facilities ensuring that every hour of every day they are consuming the cheapest fuel option among oil, propane, and natural gas. We dispatch their facilities, act as agent to buy/sell natural gas, arrange transportation, schedule the gas, and reconcile with pipes and customers.
