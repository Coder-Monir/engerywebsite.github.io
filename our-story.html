Our Stroy

We are also exceptionally proud of GIVETOLIVE, a charitable organization founded within the walls and by the employees of Energy Atlantica. Over 14 years, our team has created, organized, directed, and participated in endurance events that have inspired thousands of people to exercise, give back, and achieve something extraordinary. Collectively, these efforts have raised over $3.5 million for worthy causes.

2005

​Energy Atlantica founded and incorporated. EA hires its first employee..

2006

Trading floor launched – commercial trading of natural gas begins, fulfilling the needs of large ​industrial users and producers..

2007

GIVETOLIVE founded by the employees of Energy Atlantica. The first BIG RIDE raises over $300,000 for cancer research.

2008

$100M in trading volume achieved

2009

​EA expands to trade in the US

2011

The first BIG SWIM is organized, a 20km ocean swim across the Northumberland Strait, from New Brunswick to PEI.

2018

​GIVETOLIVE surpasses $3m in funds raised for charitable causes

2019

$1M raised by BIG SWIM to send chronically ill children to camp and Brigadoon Village honours GIVETOLIVE, naming the BIG SWIM dock GIVETOLIVE surpasses $3m in funds raised for charitable causes.

2020

Energy Atlantica celebrates its 15th Anniversary
